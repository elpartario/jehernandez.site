Double Refraction

Double Refractionwas a work that explored the effects of social technology through a unique blending of music and choreography. The work was made in collaboration with Genene Wallis-McGrath, who choreographed and performed the work. I used the wearable music technology/instruments MiMu Gloves and Instrument of Things’ SOMI-1 Bands to create a work where the performer (a keyboardist) and a dancer/choreographer were totally dependent on each other to create sound.
