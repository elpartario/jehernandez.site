In Flanders Fields

In Flanders Fields- A Veterans Day multi-media film project.

I co-directed this program which includes music by Maurice Ravel (his piano trio), as well as my own piece, SHEER. I also produced the film and recorded the music/video of the performance.

Rienzi, the MFAH house museum for European decorative arts, partnered with the University of Houston’s Moores School of Music for “In Flanders Fields,” a special performance to honor Veterans Day.
