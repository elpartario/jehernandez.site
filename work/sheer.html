SHEER

SHEER - For Piano Trio.

SHEERwas commissioned by the Museum of Fine Arts Houston along with the University of Houston’s Moores School of Music. The music is inspired by the letters of WWI poet Wilfred Owen. The piece premiered in a multi-media film project which I co-directed along with Jonathan Scofield. The program was titled In Flanders Fields. Read more about the whole project here.
