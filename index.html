J.E. Hernández

J.E. Hernández (b. 1993) is a Mexican-born, San Diego/Houston-based composer, cinematographer, researcher and ethnographer whose practice bridges inner and outer genealogies through sound and multi-disciplinary work. His music has been featured by the Tanglewood Music Center, the Kennedy Center, Lincoln Center, Opera Theatre of Saint Louis, Houston Grand Opera, the Contemporary Arts Museum Houston, and in a wide variety of films; recent projects include Ilnamikilispan, ’Ux Winal, and East End Echoes Vol. 1. He is currently pursuing a PhD in Music Composition at UC San Diego.
